Chicken

Description:

Chicken breasts has been marinated in soy sauce, ginger, lemon juice, sriracha hot sauce, salt, pepper, paprika, buttermilk and pickle juice. After marinating overnight, the chicken breasts have been tosses in the oven to cook it only half way. Now for the creamy and spicy alfredo sauce. First we pour milk into a pot and bring it up to just a light bubble. We then turn it down to a light heat and start adding the cheese. Keep stirring until it becomes the correct consistency. Season it with a little salt and pepper and some chili flakes. The sauce is now complete, so then we toss our chicken breasts and let it finish in the sauce. Last crucial step is to ENJOY!

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • American Cheese
  • Whole milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Sriracha hot sauce
  • Lemon Juice
  • Buttermilk
  • Pickle Juice
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger
